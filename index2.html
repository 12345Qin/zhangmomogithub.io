About them and their music

Seven-member South Korean boy band BTS also known as Bangtan Boys has only debuted eight years ago but they rewriting K-Pop history with every move.They are the first Korean musicians to enter the UK Albums Chart and break records with their released album"WINGS".They started out in a small company with no capital and heavy liabilities,and went to the world through their efforts.Now the company has a market value of 1 trillion won,closely following the three major entertainment companies in South Korea,which is the Korea idol industry myth.

They received three BBMA awards and performances,AMA finals,three famous Talk Shows in the United States,American Gold Records,Japanese platinum records,Youtube's 100 million playback volume world record,at United Nations Conference 7-minute speeches,TIME Weekly Asian Edition Coverman,and the only idol group in South Korea won the Cultural Medal in 2019.2019.5.2,BTS won the Billboard Best Group Award,becoming the first Asian group to win the BBMA's Best Group Award.
